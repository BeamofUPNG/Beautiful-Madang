The Story of Beautiful Madang

My First Paragraph.

Madang is a legendary name given to one of most the beautiful place you could ever find in the Country of Papua New Guinea. The name of Madang was used by the Papuan Natives who had accompanied the German administrators after their home island and only become their official name of the settlement towards the end of the German administrations. As the generations is increasing, they came up with a definition of the initial for MADANG which defined as "Memories And Dreams Are Never Gone".

From the South Coast of Madang, you could feel the cool breeze blowing from the mountains down to the down to the valley, into the tremendous rain forest of Wali village. You could hear the beautiful voices of the birds dancing and singing like Rihanna in the deep forest up along the wonderful Panim waterall wetlands to the biggest Gum river that is the source of water supply in Madang Town. As you go up the forest or down the waterfall, you will see the kunai grass and the beautiful nature of Kamba Kauris, Braim, Oppi, and all the other villages in the South side will sway to the beats of the winds.
